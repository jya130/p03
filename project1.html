Citymapper Feature Design

idea

Project Brief

In an interaction design course, I worked with a team of three to design a new feature for a transit application Citymapper. The first stage of this project includes user research and ethnography research where I conducted a face-to-face interview with a novice user to understand and observe how users interact with the application.
